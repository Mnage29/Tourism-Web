Bhandardara Dam

Bhandardara has a number of treking and hiking routes,[citation needed] including a trek to Ratangad fort, which is reached by boat over a small lake near the village Ratanwadi.[citation needed]Ratangad is a fortress situated at the edge of ghats with a number of water cisterns and caves. It also commands views all around and is said to be the favourite fort of the Maratha king, Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.[citation needed]
